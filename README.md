The online education market is flooded with options — over 200,000 courses across major platforms alone. Most career advice about certifications is either too generic ("just get certified!") or sponsored by the platforms selling them.

This guide cuts through the noise with data-backed recommendations, practical frameworks, and honest assessments of what works and what doesn't.
